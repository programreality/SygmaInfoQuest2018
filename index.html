About College

One of the leading Business Management Colleges in Mangaluru,NAAC re-accredited with 'A' Grade[2017], affiliated to Mangalore University, pioneer in Management education of undergraduate level, established and managed under the Patronage of Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara Education Society, Ujire (D.K). The Institution focuses on preparing students to face the global business challenges. SYGMA is a state level IT Fest organised by Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara College of Business Management Mangalore to provide a platform for budding IT professionals and technology enthusiasts to gain real world experience and showcase their skills.

INFOQUEST

“If you think technology can solve your security problems, then you don’t understand the problems and you don’t understand the technology”, DATA is the most vital asset for any organization or Institution. It is the basic building block of everything we do, the reports we build, the analysis we perform, the decision we Influence and the optimization we derive. In today’s scenario, we are living in a world of BIG DATA, which keeps on multiplying every second. Without ample resources and tools for organizing and managing data, organization will fail miserably owing to the competition in today’s work.

The theme chosen for SYGMA 2018 is INFOQUEST, which provides you a series of challenges to prove your mettle. So be there to witness a plethora of excitement and extravaganza at the SYGMA 2018 INFOQUEST.
